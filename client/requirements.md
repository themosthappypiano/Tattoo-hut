## Notes
Dynamic images are served from /images/
Gallery data comes from GET /api/gallery
Fonts: 'Cinzel' for headings (script-like/display), 'Inter' for body
Color Palette:
- Background: #111111 (rich black)
- Surface: #1A1A1A (dark grey)
- Accent: #FF2200 (neon red/orange)
- Chrome: #C0C0C0 (silver)
- Text: #FFFFFF (white)
